P&B, Interactive store

Big screen displays in stores to show off the seasonal Lookbooks, clothing and social live share

Back to Home
Rich experience before, during and after shopping

To improve the shopping experience in the main P&B flagstore in Spain located in A Coruña, we developed several big screens with different kinds of experiences looking for engage with clients, cultivate the process of buying and develop a strong rich brand.

Customers became the main characters of the scene in "Pull your look" fashion retail experience. They could interact with the store, with the screens, with the LEDs. They could share with other customers their lookbooks in the "totems" and in the brand's Facebook. They could share with their friends and family their new looks in social networks.

The "Pull your look" experience

The process started before entering the store with a big screen facing the pedestrians, whom could interact with the screen as the video animation followed the movements of the people on the street.

Once the client enters the store, the interactive "totems" screens helped the customer to search clothes and showcase examples of different preselected combinations: the Lookbooks. Customers were able to search the new season products, see prices, check availabity (all over the spanish state P&B stores), and examples that fit their selected items.

Finally customers could share in Facebook how the clothes fitted them and share their photos with their new look.

"Pull your look" interactive store experience was exported to several latin american countries and get a 30% increase of permanency in P&B stores.
