Using 3D as an asset

I love how new technologies develop in order to expand the limits of design and makes it more flexible and easy to accomplish the goal expected. My skills in 3D are getting better everyday, and my curve of learning is increasing more and more. The use of a 3D software such as Cinema 4D helps me to enhance my process of work and, the most important thing, helps me to produce the images that I had in my head and wasn't able to reproduce previously with my 2D software knowledge.

The Z Axis for the 2D

Lately I discovered how 3D could improve 2D designs. In a nutshell, I discovered that a basic knowledge in 3D software can improve exponentially on how show things in 2D designs. It makes watching the design in a new, fresh way. That's why nowadays 3D doesn't mean just modelling, lighting or texturing. It could mean also enhancing.

This project's name is "Gridcore"
