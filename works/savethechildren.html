Save the Children

Responsive multi-media scrollable website with editorial approach at its core.

Back to Home

Save the Children's Signature Programmes showcase the work the charity does in aim to help young kids to preserve their basic rights as health, basic education and, in top of everything, its own survival.

The responsive web app shows the stories behind the programmes in a compelling digital storytelling format, with the objective of communicate Signature Programmes as an attractive source to get funds to.

As an international project, the web app is multilingual and completely multimedia, featuring text, quotes, video, interactive map and background ambience sound. Everything helps to achieve the credibility and the impact for a great editorial storytelling digital experience.

The project started as an internal tool for Save the Children's staff. However, finally became opened to everyone, preserving a private section for the staff accessing at the end of the last chapter. In addition, the site is fully controllable via CMS allowing Save the Children to be able to customise any content at any time.

A full responsive approach was applied and all the multimedia assets were optimised for each device and screen sizes.
