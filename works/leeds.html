The Metropolitan University is now Leeds Beckett

The old Metropolitan University of Leeds was renamed as Leeds Beckett University and needed a new website and digital identity in order to have a consistent communication. The main objective was to create a modern digital design and clean layout that could engage with the young and modern student to keep the website as a handy tool in a day-to-day basis.

The complexity of the information architecture made the UX a key part of the project. Slick design and interactions helped achieve that purpose. Mobile first approach helped create the website with the most pure and simplest information in mind at all times.
