Red Bull Bedroom Jam

Digital platform and campaign contest for young bands.

Back to Home

Red Bull needed to evolve its platform 'Bedroom Jam' to a whole new level. This time the platform require an international launching and a complete new design and UI in order to fulfill the needs of the younger users worldwide and unroll the site to a multi-device and responsive solution.

The new strategy was to make both, bands and fans, the center of the action within the platform. Bands needed to 'make noise' in social media, while fans could get rewarded for supporting the bands back.

Mobile first approach, functionalities 'on demand', off-canvas navigation, parallax scrolling and a powerful predictive search were the key functionalities to make the user experience optimised and produce a clean modern look and feel.

With rollouts in Belgium, Greece and more to come, Red Bull Bedroom Jam is ready to take the world by storm and help new bands rise above the static.
