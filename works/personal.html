A bigger company, a better company.

James Grant had an urgent need to review all its brands as a multi-brand group and contain all of their communications in a singular platform so it's understood as a genuine brand living under the same url direction and with a consistent look and feel. The new website will be a big container for all communications from within the James Grant Group, all along the talent, sub-brands, industries and even news.

Different brands within James Grant Group, with its own colour code were hosted in the new website platform. They preserve their own style, with the advantage of being a parent under the main JG structe, look and feel and domain.

Key aspects we took care of

Navigation became a key functionality in order to preserve the order and information hierarchy inside the website.
Specific functionality will be displayed depending on the section the user is in.

James Grant users and stakeholders are in high demand for a website accessible from their mobile, tablets and other devices.

The new company brand should be consistent with the responsive approach we followed in the project. Grabbing existing elements of the main brand, the new James Grant logo minimised gets adapted with this idea in mind, optimising the branding even in the smallest situations.
