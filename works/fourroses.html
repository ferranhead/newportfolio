Bourbon is for girls too

The bourbon brand Four Roses realized they needed an increase in feminine consumers. We decided that Dazzle, one of the main characters we developed previously in "Four Roses, the comic" could do a great job representing the women we were searching for.

The design style followed the comic style to blend all communications into a single one and turn all brand messages consistent.

The first streaming tattoo video on Facebook

The campaign consisted in a contest where women completed their entry in a Facebook tab to have the chance to win a pack of Four Roses bourbon and get tattoed live and in streaming on Facebook. The winner proved her commitment with the brand getting a rose tattoo to not forget Four Roses is for girls. For tough girls.
